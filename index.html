Where It All Began

Learning should never feel like a chore. I built these apps because I wanted Leo Park to have a place where numbers, letters, and clocks come alive — where every tap feels like an adventure, not a test. Each app was designed to meet Leo exactly where he is, spark a little joy, and let him discover things at his own pace.

They are simple. They are small. But every pixel was placed with care.
