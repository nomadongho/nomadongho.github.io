For Parents & Caregivers

What does this app teach?

  • Basic Te Reo Māori words: greetings, numbers, colors, family, animals, body parts, feelings
  • Pronunciation helpers for each word
  • Gentle listening-first approach – great for ages 4–7

How to use it together:

  • Sit with your child and listen to words together
  • Encourage them to repeat the word out loud
  • Celebrate every attempt – getting it wrong is fine!
  • Audio repetition works best: listen, repeat, listen again

Note: This is a gentle introductory app. It is not a complete language course. Think of it as a warm, fun first taste of Te Reo Māori.
